Round 2 Submission Focus


As we move into this phase, the focus shifts from broad ideas to deep execution.
Instead of building a full product, participants must:
Identify ONE core feature and build it end-to-end with full functionality
What We Expect
* A complete full-stack implementation
* A single-user flow (UI → blockchain interaction → transaction confirmation)
* Direct integration with Algorand blockchain
* A working product, not a prototype
Note: Clickable prototypes or partial demos will NOT be accepted.
Example
If you're building in Future of Finance:
* Focus on just lending flow OR
* Just staking mechanism
* But ensure it works perfectly from user click to on-chain execution.
* You can leverage tools like VibeKit to accelerate development.
