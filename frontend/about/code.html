Our Story

Founded in 2018, Serene began as a rejection of the "attention economy." We saw a digital world cluttered with noise, pop-ups, and frantic design patterns that prioritized metrics over humans.

Our journey started in a small studio overlooking the misty coastline of the Pacific Northwest. The landscape taught us about the power of the void—the importance of what is left out.

"Beauty is not when there is nothing more to add, but when there is nothing left to take away."
